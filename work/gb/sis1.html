ABOUT

The son of a Sicilian pizza baker on the run from a small town in Sweden. Giovanni Bucchieri. Teenage crushes: Hip hop, b-boying, Kate Bush. Fast-forward: The Wu-Tang Clan, high heels and gender-bending choreography on NYC’s Vogue scene. Back home again: Graduation from the Royal Swedish Ballet School. 24 hour clubbing in Stockholm. Champaigne and Kool-Aids.

Then, the 00’s: The Royal Swedish Ballet at the Stockholm Opera, the Cullberg ballet, the 59North dance troupe. At the Opera, Giovanni . The performance piece Come you daughters (choreography Bucchieri, evolves into something bigger. Sister is born : The debut record ”If I’m broke I order oysters and hope a pearl will pay my bill”

from Sister,, is out now on digital channels. Soothing children’s voices clash with heavy rhythms in a sumptous, orchestral arrangement – imagine The Knife ditching their sequencers and hiring Arcade Fire as a back-up band. The war is over from record is featured as the main song on the soundtrack to Swedish director Anna Odell’s award-winning feature film The Reunion, which premiered at the Venice Film Festival earlier this year. Come, you daughters.

Before becoming an musician and actor many regarded Giovanni Bucchieri as one of the most talented Swedish dancers of his generation.

He is also a self-taught classical pianist, composer, film director, choreographer and singer. Operating is such a wide field; Bucchieri could be described as the classical cultural polymath.

Giovanni Bucchieri spent his youth at Pizzeria Romana in Linköping. He established himself early as a prolific dancer, specialized in breakdance and in Street dance. At the age of 15 he moved to Stockholm. He traveled to New York at the age of 16. There he eagerly took part in the frantic club life of New York. It was here that Bucchieri’s love for Vogue was discovered, at various ballroom dances. It was also here that the seeds were sawn for his invented dance style Horse. Horse is a dance style closely connected with Greek elegies and modern Vogue. The style was created by Bucchieri as a reaction to, and as an antidote to, the drugs. As in the elegies of the Greek dramas, in Horse a kind of trans is reach. The dance is a highly stylized dance, through which stories and emotions -which are to difficult and taboo to talk about – are expressed.

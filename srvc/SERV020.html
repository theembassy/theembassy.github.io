  • A compilation of Jens' three first Ep:s plus unreleased tracks. It's all here: the classic debut "Maple Leaves Ep", the strictly limited edition "Rocky Dennis in Heaven Ep" and "Julie Ep". There's the smash Black Cab, the timeless masterpiece Maple Leaves, the lonely crystal sound of Jens Lekman's Farewell Song, the genius of a song based on a sample of a downloaded bootleg of an unauthorized Jens-show leeked onto the internet (Hammer Hill), lovesick cats screaming (F-word) and so on.

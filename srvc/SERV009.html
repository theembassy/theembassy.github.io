  • A truly extensive debut EP that shows off Jens´s different modes. Both big, sample based arangements and intimate acoustic tracks. Pocketful of Money is loosely based on Beat Happening´s Gravediggers Blues and Someone to Share My Life With is a TV Personalities cover. The EP hit big. It spent 22 weeks on the singles chart and peaked at no 11. Both Maple Leaves and Black Cab were radio hits.
